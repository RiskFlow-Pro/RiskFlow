04 — Ladder Orders — Auto DCA

DCA across 2–4 levels,
risk distributed.

Ladder mode builds an entry plan across 2, 3 or 4 independent Limit orders. Weights are automatically distributed in increasing fashion toward the SL.

🤖
Auto weights increasing toward SL

Level L1 (furthest from SL) carries the lowest weight; subsequent levels carry progressively higher weights to lower the average entry.

🛑
Shared SL across all levels

A single Stop Loss is attached to all orders. Total risk stays as defined — it doesn't multiply with the number of levels.

📐
Per-level size formula

For each level: size_i = (risk × weight_i) / sl_distance_i — proportional to the risk allocated to that specific level.

⚡
All orders sent in one shot

After confirmation, all levels are placed on the exchange simultaneously. Margin mode and leverage configured automatically.

05 — Risk Management

SL Shield &
Daily Loss Limit.

Two operational discipline tools that act at different levels: the first protects the individual trade, the second protects the entire session.

  • SL Shield — prevents widening the stop loss on open positions. You can only move it toward entry (reducing risk), never away from it.
  • refSL — SL value confirmed by the exchange at the moment of opening. Shield compares every modification against this reference.
  • Daily Loss Limit (DLL) — set a daily drawdown cap in %. When triggered, the terminal locks all orders for 24h.
  • Persistent lock — DLL lock persists after refresh, browser close, tab change. Not bypassable.
  • Breakeven with one click — automatically moves SL to entry price. Zero residual risk on the open position.
